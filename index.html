I've used programs like photoshop, canva, and adobe express to design posters for myself or friends. I look forward to revisiting programs like these, and having the chance to learn more of the principles of design to create more visually pleasing and interesting work.

What is good design?

I think good design can encapsulate any number of design choices, so long as the end result is visually pleasing, easy to interact with as a user, and stands the test of common sense. When I practice my graphic design, I like to make choices that I think will show the viewer how I see things. I'll use bold and contrasting colors, several differnet patterns, overlapping images, and often try to make cultural references that I think fit.
